Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2025
✓ No personal data collected or shared

This Privacy Policy describes how the YouTrack to Sheets Chrome extension ("the Extension") handles information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

What the Extension Does

YouTrack to Sheets reads issue data from the YouTrack page you are currently viewing (such as issue ID, title, status, priority, author, assignee, and dates) and appends it as a new row to a Google Spreadsheet of your choice, using your own Google account.

Data We Collect

The Extension reads the following data only from the active YouTrack tab, and only when you explicitly click the export button:

  • Issue ID, title, URL
  • Issue status, priority
  • Author and assignee names (as displayed on the page)
  • Issue creation date

This data is read directly from the page DOM and is never stored, logged, or transmitted to any server other than the Google Sheets API endpoint you configured.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personally identifiable information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • No browsing history or page content outside of YouTrack issue pages
  • No authentication credentials, passwords, or tokens
  • No location data
  • No analytics or usage tracking of any kind

Google Account & OAuth

The Extension uses Google OAuth 2.0 via the Chrome Identity API to obtain a temporary access token for the Google Sheets API. This token is used solely to write issue data to the spreadsheet you specify. The Extension does not store, share, or transmit your Google credentials or OAuth tokens anywhere.

Local Storage

The Extension saves the following settings locally in your browser using chrome.storage.sync:

  • Google Spreadsheet ID (entered by you)
  • Target sheet name
  • Preferred interface language (EN/RU)
  • Export field order and visibility preferences

These settings are synced across your Chrome profile via Google's built-in sync mechanism. No third parties have access to this data.

Third-Party Services

The Extension communicates exclusively with the Google Sheets API (sheets.googleapis.com) to append rows to your spreadsheet. No other third-party services, analytics platforms, or external servers are contacted.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties. Issue data written to your Google Spreadsheet is governed by Google's own privacy policy and terms of service.

Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in the extension's GitHub repository or contact us at the email listed in the Chrome Web Store listing.
